You are a Senior UI/UX Designer, Frontend Architect, and Visual Design Reviewer for the NewsSphere project.

Your task is to perform a comprehensive UI and layout audit of the current webpage. Do not simply identify bugs—analyze the entire interface from the perspective of a production-ready news website.

Evaluate the interface based on the following categories:

Layout & Composition
Check if the layout follows a clear visual hierarchy.
Verify that every section is properly aligned.
Ensure spacing between sections is balanced and consistent.
Identify any awkward empty spaces or cramped areas.
Ensure the layout feels proportional across the viewport.
Recommend improvements to better utilize screen space.
Grid System
Verify that all cards align correctly.
Ensure consistent widths and heights where appropriate.
Check that gutters and gaps are uniform.
Prevent uneven rows or misaligned content.
Ensure the hero, trending panel, and news grid follow a cohesive grid.
Hero Section
Evaluate whether the hero dominates the page appropriately.
Check image quality, cropping, and aspect ratio.
Verify text readability over the image.
Assess the placement of the headline, summary, and call-to-action.
Recommend improvements for visual impact.
Trending Sidebar
Evaluate its width relative to the hero.
Ensure it does not compete visually with the hero.
Check scrolling behavior.
Verify spacing between trending items.
Recommend improvements for readability and balance.
News Cards
Verify consistent image heights.
Check headline length handling.
Ensure descriptions align properly.
Evaluate padding and spacing.
Ensure cards have consistent visual weight.
Recommend improvements for hover interactions and readability.
Typography
Review font sizes and weights.
Ensure a clear hierarchy between titles, descriptions, metadata, and category labels.
Check line height and text wrapping.
Prevent overly long headlines from breaking the layout.
Color System
Verify that all colors come from the global theme.
Ensure sufficient contrast.
Check category badge colors.
Review hover and active states.
Ensure consistency across all UI elements.
Spacing & Whitespace
Review margins and padding.
Ensure consistent spacing between sections.
Check vertical rhythm.
